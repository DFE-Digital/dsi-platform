Users can be associated with multiple organisations at the same time. Each organisations service access is handled on its own.

To access a service for different or multiple organisations, you need the service added to each organisation.

By selecting the 'Show organisations' drop down on your [My services page](${{ SERVICES_URL }}my-services),
you can see which organisations you have a service added to.

Learn how to [request access to a service](/your-account/request-service-access).

If you need further assistance, please contact the support team through our [contact us form](/contact-us).
